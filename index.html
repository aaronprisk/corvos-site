About

CorvOS is free Linux distribution focused on K-12 schools. The name derives from the latin word corvus, the scientific name for the Raven and Crow family of a birds.

CorvOS takes the best attributes of a traditional Linux desktop: Speed, Security, Flexibility and merges it with the Simplicity of ChromeOS without the privacy issues and Google vendor lock-in.

As of today, there are over 3000 and counting laptops and desktops all over the world running CorvOS. Check out The Flock page to find out where and how it's being used.

Stable Xubuntu Base

CorvOS is based on Xubuntu 18.04, the phenomenal XFCE powered operating system.

Basing CorvOS on Xubuntu ensures a solid, trusted base that will recieve updates for multiple years.

To learn more about Xubuntu, check out their website: Xubuntu.org

Custom Student Profile

CorvOS has a self cleaning student profile that ensures a consistent experience for student users.

Upon logoff, the system automatically cleans the home directory and any web browsing data that was created during that session.
